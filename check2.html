Energy Meter

Arduino-Based Energy Meter The Arduino based energy meter consists of an Arduino Uno board, current sensor, voltage sensor, relay, GSM module, LCD screen and AC/DC power supply. An AC to DC power supply is used to convert the AC power to a DC power to power the system. The voltage sensor measures the voltage of the source while the current sensor measures the power consumption. A microcontroller embedded onto the Arduino Uno board analyse and interpret the information it receives from the current and voltage sensor and compute the corresponding energy consumed. The computed energy and other variables would be displayed by the LCD screen. The purpose of the relay is to control the supply of power to the consumer. The GSM module is to ensure the connection between the meter and a server for communication.

Mobile App

The android mobile application has been designed to provide a functional and easy-to-use tool to the energy consumers to monitor, manage and control the energy meter, and also pay their energy bills. The application was developed using Android Studio and Android software development kit (SDK) and Kotlin programming. The graphical interface consisted of the meter management menu, the make payment menu, view consumption menu, monitor consumption menu, make complaints menu and notifications menu. The meter management menu is designed to help the consumer add meter to the system and also delete meter from the application, switch to a particular meter in the case of consumers having multiple meters and also switch between consumer modes; either from prepaid to postpaid or vice versa provided the necessary requirement are met.
